# cqurec.github.io

Wei Zhou

Wei Zhou is a Professor at the School of Big Data and Software Engineering, Chongqing University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Chongqing University in 2015. Prior to his current faculty position, he held research appointments at the University of Auckland, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), CAS. His research expertise lies in recommender systems, social computing, and data mining. Prof. Zhou has authored over 80 peer-reviewed articles in prestigious venues, including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TSC, IEEE TCSS, ACM TOIS, and top-tier conferences such as IJCAI and SIGIR.
